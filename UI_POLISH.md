# UI polish notes

Contrast and depth updates in `src/styles.css` (May 2026).

## Text & borders

- Darker `--text-muted` and `--text-tertiary` for readable body copy on pink surfaces.
- `text-coral` uses `--coral-ink` for links/icons; `bg-coral` unchanged for nav pills and buttons.
- Stronger border tokens for hairlines on cards and tables.

## Shadows

Shared scale: `shadow-xs`, `shadow-sm`, `shadow-card`, `shadow-action`, `shadow-elevated`, `shadow-depth`.

## Focus

Global `focus-visible` rings on links, buttons, inputs, and tables.
